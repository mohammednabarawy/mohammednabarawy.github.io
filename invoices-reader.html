Three Powerful Processing Modes

Choose the best method for your needs - from fastest to most private

ZATCA E-Invoice QR Code

The fastest and most reliable method. Instantly reads ZATCA-compliant QR codes to extract all invoice details with 100% accuracy.

  • Instant processing
  • Best for:
  • • ZATCA-compliant Saudi e-invoices
  • • When speed and accuracy are critical
  • • High-volume processing

Gemini AI Cloud Processing

Uses Google's powerful Gemini AI to analyze invoice layouts and extract data. Works with any invoice format but requires internet.

  • Requires internet
  • Best for:
  • • Non-ZATCA invoices
  • • Complex or unusual invoice formats
  • • When you need good accuracy without QR codes

Local AI Processing

Runs Ollama AI models locally on your PC. Protects sensitive data as nothing leaves your computer. Speed depends on your hardware.

  • Maximum privacy
  • Best for:
  • • Highly confidential invoices
  • • When internet isn't available
  • • Users with powerful PCs (GPU recommended)
You can switch between modes anytime based on your needs
